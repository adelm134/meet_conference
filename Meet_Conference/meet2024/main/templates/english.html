MEET.STUDENTS

A dedicated conference for students to showcase their research, innovation and ideas in the fields of medicine, engineering, education and technology.



MEET.EXPO

An exhibition showcasing innovative projects, products and services in the fields of medicine, engineering, education and technology, providing participants with the opportunity to gain hands-on experience and network.



MEET.TALKING

An exciting session in the Science Slam format, a non-academic form of scientific communication, is a competition in the form of short speeches by scientists with stories about their scientific research in popular science form on interesting scientific topics, including the use of artificial intelligence.



MEET.SCIENCE

A scientific conference with presentations, workshops and discussions on cutting-edge research and development in various fields.



MEET.AWARDS

An awards ceremony where participants will be able to evaluate and select their favorite works. Each participant will be able to vote for submitted research, innovation and projects in the fields of medicine, engineering, education and technology. The work with the most votes will receive a prize and special recognition at the conference.
